Privacy Statement

Welcome to our Chrome extension! We are committed to protecting your privacy and adhere to the highest standards of data protection and relevant laws and regulations. Before using our extension, please read this Privacy Statement carefully to understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information Collection

Our Chrome extension runs locally, and all data is stored on your local device. No data is sent to any external servers or third-party services. We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information or sensitive information.

Permission Explanation

  • contextMenus: Allows us to add functionality to the browser's right-click menu for convenient use of our extension features.
  • storage: Allows us to store data on your local device so that it remains available even after your browser is closed.
  • activeTab: Allows us to interact with the currently active tab to capture URL and browsing position data.
  • scripting: Allows us to inject custom scripts into web pages to record browsing position data.
  • tabs: Allows us to view and modify browser tabs to implement quick navigation functionality.
  • notifications: Allows us to send notifications to you to alert you of specific events.

Use of Information

The information we collect is used only for providing feature support. We use stored URL information and browsing position data to enable you to quickly access web pages you have previously visited and restore to previous browsing positions.

Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any personal information or extension usage data to any third parties.

Information Security

We take all reasonable measures to protect the security of your personal information and extension usage data and strictly adhere to relevant security standards and best practices.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect our business needs, legal requirements, or technological changes. When we make significant changes to the Privacy Policy, we will notify you through appropriate means.
